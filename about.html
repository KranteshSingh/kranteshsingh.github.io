What we do

Since 2007, we have helped 25 companies launch over 1k incredible growth

BRINC

Yuktix was among the 11 startups which were selected by BRINC Poland Prize (Hardware & IoT) & Scale-Up (Industrial IoT) accelerator programs.

C-CAMP

Yuktix is part of second cohort of 14 Centre for Cellular and Molecular Platforms (C-CAMP) Bio-startups under the Karnataka Bio-Startup Advancement Program (K-SAP BIO 50).

CEATEC, Japan, 2016

Yuktix was part of a top 10 IoT startups delegation participating in CEATEC 2016, the largest exhibition and trade fair for advanced electronics and IT in Japan. The trip was organized by METI ( Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry in Japan).

India Israel Innovation Bridge Winner

Yuktix was runner up of India-Israel Innovation Bridge 2018 under the Agriculture Challenge 1: Solution for reducing post harvest loss and improving market linkage.

Karnataka Idea2POC Winner

The Idea2PoC is a scheme of Dept. of IT, BT and S&T, Govt. of Karnataka. The scheme enables technology innovators and entrepreneurs to pursue a promising technology idea, and validate proof of concept (POC). yuktix received a grant for developing wireless sensor network for agriculture monitoring idea into a viable PoC (proof of concept)

NASSCOM Emerge 50

Yuktix was selected as one of the top 50 start-ups by NASSCOM under Emerge 50 2015 for Agriculture disease prediction system using IoT devices.
